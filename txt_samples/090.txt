So the presentation is about high school education in America. It talks about the history of high school which started in America from 1607 until now. Then it talks about the education system in America where American students go to class between 7 or 9 am in the morning, then return from school around 4 pm. Also, curriculum activities that students can choose including swimming, tracking, running, and etc. Then American students spend almost 120 hours in lessons in class each year. Besides that, education in America has an impact. The positive impact is that American education gives students chances to succeed and social mobility. The negative impact is the educational gap between social and economic students and also when students feel stressed and anxious because of the high pressure for high academic standards.
