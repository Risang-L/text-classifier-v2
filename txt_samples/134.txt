The American film industry has become a long way in history and is full of innovation. In the early 20th century, major studios such as Warner Bros, Paramount, and MGM began to flourish, producing milestone classics casablanca (1942). Post world war II, Hollywood reacged a golden age with the emergence of new genres, such as The Godfather (1972) and Taxi Driver(1976).American actors such as Marlon Brando, James dean, and Marilyn Monroe became pop culture icons that influenced global lifestyle. Behind the scenes, professions such as directors, screenwriters, and film editors are key to the industry's sucess. To this day, Hollywood remains the center of the world's largest film industry, producing worldwide blockbusters such as star wars and avengers, giving the united states a significant economic and cultural impact.
