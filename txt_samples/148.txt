Education at the high school level is an important phase in a student's life, during which they not only learn academic material, but also experience significant personal growth. This paper will explore various aspects of high school education in America, including its rich history, curriculum implemented, and the various types of scheduling used. With a deeper understanding of how high schools have developed over time, as well as the diversity of existing curricula and scheduling, it is hoped that it can provide valuable insight for education stakeholders, both within. or outside of class.
