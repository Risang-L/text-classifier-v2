The American film industry has long been one of the most largest and most influential in the world. Hollywood, a leading center of film production, has produced many popular movies that are loved by audiences around the world, such as Star Wars, Avengers, Titanic and The Shawshank Redemption. Talented actors from the United States, such as Tom Hanks, Meryl Streep, Leonardo DiCaprio, and Scarlett Johansson, have become icons on the big screen with brilliant careers and stunning achievements, both through prestigious awards and impressive box office. The American film industry not only produces quality entertainment, but also has a significant economic impact, providing jobs for many and being one of the most important cultural assets for the United States.
