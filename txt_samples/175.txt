United States high school culture in no less interesting. Diverse backgrounds and interests combine, creating a unique cultural mosaic. There are those who are popular in the academic field, there are those who shine on the sports field, there are those who dedicate themselves to art and music, and there are also those who are active in social organizations. Each individual has their own place, respected and valued for their uniqueness. However, behind the glitter of American high school life, there are also challenges that students must face. Academic pressure, bullying, and personal problems can become stumbling blocks in their journey.
